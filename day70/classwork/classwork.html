Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, saut√© pancetta and garlic until crisp.
  3. Beat eggs in a bowl, then add grated Parmesan.
  4. Drain spaghetti and combine immediately with pancetta.
  5. Remove pan from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with parsley, and serve.
